# contrib/ — external drafts, preserved with provenance

Work contributed from outside this working copy, kept in one place (the
canonical tree) rather than scattered across speculative workspaces.
Nothing under contrib/ is loaded by the validator, the generated suites,
or the demos: these are records ABOUT the web's history, not grounds in it.

- bob-orbital-draft-2026-07/ — the orbital-mechanics draft and analysis
  documents from the ~/bob workspace (produced against a v0.5.0 checkout).
  Its layering architecture was adopted (examples/orbital-mechanics.ttl);
  its implementation was superseded (every joint restated its conclusion —
  the circular-provenance pattern that motivated engine/consumption.py,
  ROOT.md §15.4). REVIEW.md is the full review; README.md inside explains
  what was kept, what was rebuilt, and why the byte-identical duplicates
  were punctured rather than copied.
